# Before I Die 😭🕊️

> **A quiet, honest place to remember the things you don’t want to forget to live.**

Before I Die is not a productivity tool. It is a **life intention tracker** — built for reflection, momentum, and meaning.

---

## 🧠 Product Vision

People don’t abandon bucket lists because they don’t care.
They abandon them because life gets loud.

**Before I Die exists to keep important dreams visible, gentle, and alive — without pressure.**

### North Star

> Help users *do more meaningful things*, not more things.

---

## 🎯 Core Problems We Solve

1. Bucket lists feel overwhelming and static
2. Goals get buried under daily responsibilities
3. Traditional apps gamify too aggressively
4. Achievements lack emotional closure
5. Reflection is missing from goal completion

---

## 🧩 Product Pillars

### 1. Emotional First, Functional Second

* Design for feeling before efficiency
* Calm UI, intentional spacing, slow animations

### 2. Gentle Accountability

* No loud streaks
* No punishment
* Soft reminders, seasonal nudges

### 3. Progress Without Pressure

* Visible movement without urgency
* Completion is optional, reflection is encouraged

### 4. Identity & Memory

* Goals are part of life stories
* Completed items become memories, not checkmarks

---

## 🚀 Feature Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Now)

**Goal: Capture dreams beautifully**

* Create unlimited life lists
* Emoji + description driven lists
* Add activities with flexible status
* Visual progress indicators
* Light / Dark theme with system sync
* Responsive web-first design

---

### Phase 2 — Reflection & Meaning

**Goal: Make completion emotionally satisfying**

* Completion reflection prompts
* Optional notes & photos per activity
* “What changed after this?” prompts
* Memory-style completed view
* Timeline of completed dreams

---

### Phase 3 — Intelligence & Context

**Goal: Keep dreams relevant**

* Time-aware reminders (seasonal, yearly)
* “You added this a while ago” nudges
* Priority decay (old items gently resurface)
* Contextual suggestions (travel, learning, life events)

---

### Phase 4 — Social, Carefully

**Goal: Add people without pressure**

* Private-by-default lists
* Invite-only shared lists
* Silent following (no feeds by default)
* Celebrate others without comparison
* Reactions instead of comments

---

### Phase 5 — Expansion

**Goal: Make Before I Die part of daily life**

* PWA & mobile apps
* Offline-first support
* Deep links to calendar & notes
* Export life memories (PDF / timeline)
* Long-term data portability

---

## 🧪 Experimental Ideas

* “If I had one year left” mode

* Voice notes for reflections

---

## 🎨 Design & UX Principles

* Low-saturation color palette
* Soft gradients, never harsh contrast
* Generous white space
* Slow, intentional transitions
* UI disappears when content matters

**Avoid:**

* Gamified dashboards
* Competitive leaderboards
* Loud notifications

---

## 🧠 Data & Intelligence Requirements

### Signals

* Activity creation & completion
* Reflection depth
* Time-to-completion
* List revisit frequency

### Use of Data

* Improve reminder timing
* Surface meaningful older goals
* Personalize UI emphasis

**Hard rule:** No data used to pressure users.

* Weaponize mortality for engagement
* Push toxic productivity
* Create comparison anxiety
